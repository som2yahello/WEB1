WEB이란 무엇인가?

WORLD WIDE WEB, commonly know as the web, is an information system where documents and other web resources are identifed by uniform resource locators, which may be interlinked by hypertext, and are a accessible over the internet.

the resources of the www are transferred via the hypertext transfer protocol and may be accessed by users by a software application called a web browser and are published by a soft ware application called a web server
